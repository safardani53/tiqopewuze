The must-buy frozen treat at Aldi because its 10/10 see the other one that is good but

AN Aldi super-fan taste tests products so you don't have to, and his latest has pitted two frozen desserts against each other.

The TikTok user offers reviews of some of the most popular products at Aldi.

Ryan (@RyanTalksAndEats) said in a recent video: "Aldi offers different flavors of similar items, and I try them all to tell you which ones are the best."

This time around, he shared his opinions on a frozen cheesecake and a frozen pie.

In the frozen section, Ryan pulled out a Belmont frozen whipped cheesecake.

He said it's "good but very plain," adding in that he added caramel and pecans.

Rather, he suggested snagging the Belmont chocolate creme pie which he said is "10/10 delicious."

"Both have a cookie crust that is much better than the traditional graham [cracker]" he said.

SWEET SAVINGS

Both desserts were $5.99, which is much cheaper than the $7.49 for a frozen whipped cheesecake and $7.59 for the chocolate creme pie at Target.

The Aldi desserts are less expensive than the ones at Walmart, where both a whipped cheesecake and a chocolate creme pie are $6.98.
