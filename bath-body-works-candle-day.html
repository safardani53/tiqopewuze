Bath &amp; Body Works kicks off annual candle sale with snags as shoppers flood site saying 'it's not wo

SHOPPERS are scrambling to score access to the annual candle sale from Bath & Body Works.

For the past 12 years, the retail giant has celebrated Candle Day where all three-wick candles are priced at just $9.95. 

The day-long sales event begins on December 2 and ends on December 3.

However, this year reward members are given early access.

Early entry started at 6 am and will only go until 5:29 am ET on December 2.

Members will be able to score over 150 candle assortment in-store and online through the My Bath & Body Works.

Be sure to use the code CANDLEDAY at checkout for online orders to save.

Just note that there is a limit of 24 candles at the $9.95 price.

SHOPPING FRENZY 

Bath & Body Works posted on X: "CANDLEDAY. $9.95 3-wick candles. That's the tweet. You know what to do."

The single post then sent shoppers into a tizzy.

Customers flooded the X comments begging the retailer for help as they reported the site was kicking them off.

One shopper wrote: "It is broken. lol The app kicked me out and now it will not let me log back in."

Another commented: "Something is wrong with the website. Code works, but it adds the original price or something at the end. $100 in candles are showing up as $256 on the order review page."

Time was a factor as well as another X user said: "I’ve been in the virtual line for 30 minutes and the estimated time keeps changing."

Bath & Body Works directed shoppers to call 1-800-756-5005 between 6 am - 2 am ET.
