Who is Mikayla Nogueira?

Mikayla Nogueira is an American social media influencer who became popular with her makeup and beauty-related videos and tutorials on social media.

Mikayla is the first of her type of influencer in the beauty and fashion industry and she has already latched on to the sought-after person role.
