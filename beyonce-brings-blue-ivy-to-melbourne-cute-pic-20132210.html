Beyonce, Blue Ivy Matching in Melbourne-- Picture Singer, Daughter

Adorable alert! Beyoncé arrived Tuesday at Melbourne's Rod Laver Arena with her adorable, 21-month-old daughter Blue Ivy in tow.

The 32-year-old singer, who was there to kick off the Australian segment of her Mrs. Carter Show World Tour Tuesday night, flaunted her seriously-toned legs in denim short shorts and black sneakers. She paired the athletic, relaxed outfit with a chic Zoe Karssen Creme de la Creme sweatshirt, shades and a hat. She mixed up the casual look with a bold, gold AllSaints Valtari necklace.

Blue Ivy, meanwhile, sported black-and-white, just like her mommy. On Blue's tiny toddler feet? Leather flats that appear to be marked with the Gucci emblem.

Melbourne's Herald Sun says the entire arena danced and screamed to the "Crazy In Love" crooner's set list which included "Love on Top" from her most recent album, 4, and her chart-topping hits like "Irreplaceable," "Naughty Girl," and even Destiny Child's "Survivor." She is performing her next three shows in the city, then will head over to Brisbane, Sydney, Adelaide, and Perth.

The Grammy-winning singer, who's been on tour since April, will end her wildly successful journey on Dec. 22 at the Barclays Center. Beyonce's husband, Jay Z, sold his stake in the Brooklyn-based venue and The Nets in September– earning close to $1.5 million for unloading his shares.
