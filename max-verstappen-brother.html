Who Is Jason Jaxx? Max Verstappen Brother- Age Gap &amp; Family

Jason Jaxx is the younger half-brother of the two-time Formula One World champion Max Verstappen. The Dutch-Belgian racing driver’s brother Jason is from his father’s third marriage.

Even though Max Verstappen‘s parents (both are former racers) are not together, they raised him together & instill in him a passion for speed.

Verstappen, who races under the Dutch flag in F1 for Red Bull Racing, has a sister & three half-siblings; he is close with all of them.

Verstappen has been fascinated by racing since he was four years old. He won his first national championship at nine years old.

His family background is of racing, so naturally, Maxx was drawn to it and had competed in many races since childhood. He had an instructor from an early age and was more focused on racing than studying.

In Karting, Maxx won the Belgian title in 2006 and the Dutch Minimax title in 2007. He made his racing debut on January 12, 2014, in the Florida Winter Series. On February 5, 2014, he won his first formula race at Palm Beach International Raceway. 

Max joined the Red Bull Junior team in 2014. He extended his contract with the team in 2020. In 2020, he won the Anniversary Grand Prix at Silverstone despite having a rough start.

Max Verstappen Brother

The racer focused on racing, so he would spend most of his time in practice rather than studying. His parent’s influence on his upbringing and education is vast, and he often talks about their dedication and hard work that has shaped him to become a world champion racer. 

Max also has three half-siblings from his Father, Jos’ other marriages. Jos had a daughter Blue Jaye from his second marriage with Kelly van der Waal in 2014.
