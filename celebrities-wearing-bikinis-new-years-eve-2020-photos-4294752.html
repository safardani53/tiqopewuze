The world is ready to put 2020 behind us and move onto a better and brighter 2021, and many of our favorite stars are closing out this terrible year with a bang. Celebrities like Kylie Jenner, Megan Thee Stallion, Olivia Munn and more are getting ready for the New Year by stripping down into bikinis for New Year’s Eve. Of course, they shared the wealth by posting their swimsuit pics to Instagram, too!

Jada Pinkett Smith

Jada Pinkett Smith wrote, “I welcome you 2021 with open arms. I have no expectations. I’m going to be still and grateful,” alongside a photo of herself in a tiny bikini while she lounged on the beach. Clearly, she ended 2020 with a bang.

Kylie Jenner

Kylie Jenner is spending New Year’s week with Kendall Jenner, Kris Jenner and Corey Gamble in Aspen. She hit the slopes with Kendall for a jam-packed day of snowboarding, but also got some jacuzzi and pool time in, too. Kylie posted a series of photos of herself in the water, wearing a barely-there, cream-colored bikini. She also had matching sunglasses on top of her head. “Swimming into 2021,” she wrote alongside the pics — a spot-on caption!

Candice Swanepoel

Candice Swanepoel is “dancing [her] way out of 2020” in a really sexy swimsuit. The Victoria’s Secret model hit the beach in a two-piece, which included intricate straps across her midsection, on Dec. 30. She danced around in the sand, with her tanned skin glowing in the sunlight. At one point, she turned around to show off the skimpy backside of the bathing suit, and she had a huge smile on her face the entire time. Her energy is truly infectious!

Olivia Munn

For her pre-New Year’s Eve swimsuit pic, Olivia Munn wore bikini bottoms and a tiny white crop top. She sat on top of a balcony that overlooked a gorgeous background, with bright blue water for miles behind her. Olivia also rocked a floppy hat for her outdoor photo shoot, and she struck a number of sexy poses while in the look.

Megan Thee Stallion

Throughout 2020, Megan Thee Stallion hasn’t shied away from posting sexy swimsuit pics on her page — and she continued the trend while heading into New Year’s Eve. On Dec. 30, Megan rocked a multi-colored bikini and posed for pictures in front of a lavish outdoor area. Her hair was down and in curls, and she rocked a minimalistic makeup look for the natural looking pics.

Salma Hayek

Salma Hayek fans went wild when she posted a new bikini pic just ahead of New Year’s Eve. The 54-year-old was on the beach in a bright pink swimsuit, which she paired with an open button down shirt, hat and sunglasses. “Last days of 2020,” she wrote. “Never been more grateful for being healthy and in contact with nature.” Yes, we are super envious of her pictures in paradise!

Gracie McGraw

Tim McGraw and Faith Hill’s family is also enjoying a tropical getaway, and the pair’s eldest daughter, Gracie McGraw, has been posting tons of pics and videos from the trip on her Instagram page. In one shot, she rocked a high waisted bikini and bent down to pose for the camera. Gracie has been a champion for body positivity throughout 2020 and she looked fierce and confident in her sexy swimsuit!

Bella Thorne

Bella Thorne appeared to spend some time in Tulum, Mexico leading up the the New Year. She posted a series of photos of herself in a tiny, lime green bikini while hanging out at the beach. Bella soaked up the warm weather and flipped her hair around for the photo shoot. Bella is always one of our favorite bikini queens, so we’re all for her closing out the year in a swimsuit!
