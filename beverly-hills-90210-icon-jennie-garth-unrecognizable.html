Beverly Hills, 90210 icon looks unrecognizable in hot pink bikini as platinum blonde takes a swim 35

BEVERLY Hills, 90210 star Jennie Garth has shared a new video that showed her looking unrecognizable while taking a dip in an inground pool.

Jennie, 51, is most known for her starring role as Kelly Taylor on Beverly Hills, 90210 from 1990 to 2000.

Taking to her Instagram last week, the actress showed off her diving skills as she set up a camera and filmed herself while taking a dip in the pool.

The sped-up clip showed Jennie standing on the edge of her luxurious pool before lifting her arms above her head and diving in.

Jennie then rose out of the water and shook her head before running her hands through her wet blond locks.

Jennie's toned body was on full display as she got out of the water and gave a closer view of her bright pink bikini.

The video was set to Taylor Swift's Cruel Summer.

"Who doesn’t want the weekend to be over?" she asked followers in her caption, along with a hand-raising emoji.

Jennie has had a successful acting career after her initial breakout role in the primetime soap.

A couple of years after starring in 90210, she starred alongside Amanda Bynes on the sitcom What I Like About You as the Nickelodeon alum’s older sister, Val Tyler.

Toward the end of that decade, the actress reprised her Beverly Hills role on The CW’s spinoff series, which was simply titled 90210.

Recently, she played a version of herself in the mocku-series BH90210, and she starred in the 2021 holiday movie A Kindhearted Christmas with Cameron Mathison.

BETTER THAN EVER

Last year, 90210 fans gushed over how Jennie’s beauty had remained “timeless” amid celebrating her 50th birthday.

The actress had followers praising her for appearing decades younger as her friends and former co-stars celebrated her milestone birthday on social media.

To honor her longtime friend’s big birthday, Tori Spelling shared a fun and sweet tribute on her Instagram.

The 50-year-old shared a snap of the two of them in form-fitting, retro workout ensembles – pinks and yellows for Tori and shades of blue for Jennie.

Referencing Molly Shannon’s Saturday Night Live character, Tori wrote in her touching letter: “She can kick.. she can stretch… she’s 50! - Sally O’Malley… oops I mean Jennifer Eve Garth @jenniegarth. 

“You are more beautiful today than you were 30 years ago."

Jennie replied to her 9021OMG podcast co-host in the comments: “Aw. this means a lot to me momma. Miss you!”

'90s CON REUNION

Earlier this year, the stars of Beverly Hills, 90210 got together for a reunion at '90s Con.

Jennie was seen with co-stars Tori, Shannen Doherty, Jason Priestley, and Ian Ziering for the unexpected reunion.

They all looked delighted as they grouped together on the red carpet at the event.

Jennie took to social media to caption a post that read: “It was more like Friends Con Loved seeing some familiar and friendly faces this weekend.”

Jennie and Tori were seen embracing in a sweet photo, which was sure to have made fans feel nostalgic.

She also shared a slideshow of snaps from the '90s Con weekend while holding up banners of actors she hadn’t had a chance to see, including Kathy Najimy, Mark-Paul Gosselaar, and Charmed stars Rose McGowan and Holly Marie Combs.

Jennie also defended herself against accusations of a feud with Shannen.

She wrote: “ok. everyone needs to take a deep breath…1. i simply didn’t have a picture of me with shannon on my phone to add.

“2. i didn’t have access to a shannon banner backstage. Like there wasn’t one lying in the pile. 3. we had almost completely diff schedules so sadly we barely saw each other.

“4. stop making assumptions when you have absolutely no idea what you are talking about.

"5. please stop reaching to paint such a negative narrative. here and everywhere.”

Fans were delighted with the Beverly Hills, 90210 reunion, with one writing: “You were part of my adolescence. Seeing this gang together fills my heart with joy and nostalgia.”

Another said: “Yessss!!!! loving all the 90210 nostalgia! Currently binging the series.”
