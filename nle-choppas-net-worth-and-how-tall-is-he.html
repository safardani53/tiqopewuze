What is NLE Choppa's net worth?

AMERICAN music artist NLE Choppa made his mark in the rap world back in 2019.

Here's everything you need to know about the young rapper.

Who is NLE Choppa?

NLE Choppa is a rapper from Memphis, Tennessee, who became a viral sensation.

The young rapper, whose real name is Bryson Lashun Potts, rose to stardom after releasing Shotta Flow in January 2019.

The video to the track gained 10 million views in just a month, which led to a remix with rapper Blueface.

He released his debut studio album Top Shotta on August 7 the following year, featuring rap royalty like Lil Baby, Roddy Ricch and Chief Keef.

Around a month later, Choppa pledged to stop rapping about violence, saying he had more important topics to discuss and that he wanted to "spread positivity and wake people up".

The birth of his daughter, Clover Brylie Potts, in June 2020, changed his perspective on life and encouraged him "to be a better person".

He followed up his vow in October with a new project titled From Dark to Light, signifying his commitment to his new journey.

On October 20, 2023, some fans were concerned for the rapper's well-being after his mother posted a concerning message about his whereabouts.
