Emmerdale's Rebecca Sarker dubbed 'next Bond girl' as she shows off figure in strappy bikini

EMMERDALE'S Rebecca Sarker has been dubbed the 'next Bond girl' as she showed off her figure in a strappy bikini.

Rebecca, 48, plays Dr Manpreet Sharma in the ITV soap but recently enjoyed a bit of time away from the Emmerdale village for a holiday.

Taking to Instagram, Rebecca shared a snap from her sun lounger by the beach and showed off her incredible figure in the process.

The actress wore a blue, pink and red floral bikini which tied around her neck and allowed her to showcase her toned physique.

Wearing a pair of green rimmed sunglasses and with her dark hair tied back loosely, Rebecca captioned the snap: "Hola!"

She was immediately flooded with compliments from her followers, with one writing: "Looking as gorgeous as ever".
