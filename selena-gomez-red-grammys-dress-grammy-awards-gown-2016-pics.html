Swaps Blue Gown For Red Hot Number Hollywood Life

Selena never fails to impress us with her sexy style and tonight’s Grammy Awards ceremony was certainly no exception. After hitting the red carpet with her BFF Taylor Swift, 26, in a form-fitting Calvin Klein sequin blue gown that featured a plunging neckline and two sexy cutouts, Selena decided to switch things up for the main event by slipping into a red satin Cushnie et Ochs halter dress that oozed Old Hollywood glamour. The fiery frock hugged Selena curves to perfection and, like her original blue dress, also featured two cutouts on either side for an extra dose of sex appeal.

In keeping with the theme, Selena complimented her show-stopping ensembles with an equally as stunning beauty look consisting of big, voluminous curls, a neutral smokey eye look and a blush lip. We love how versatile her style is and how she can literally wear anything she wants, and still look flawless!

On top of looking drop-dead gorgeous, Selena also appeared to be having a total blast with her bestie TayTay, who just so happened to be her hot date that evening. Before the show even started, the two gave us some serious friendship goals when they shared a super adorable video of the them freaking out together over Taylor’s first Grammy win of the night. In the sweet video, Selena in her full hair and makeup, screaming and gushing over her bestie’s win for ‘Best Music Video’ for “Bad Blood.” Eventually, the camera pans to Taylor, who can’t even look straight-on in the lens, as she seems to practically be in tears. The girls share a sweet hug at the ending of the video, and it’s beyond adorable. “We just won a Grammy for the “Bad Blood” music video and we are NOT OK,” Taylor captioned the video. Seriously, how perfect are these two?


While Selena herself may not have been nominated for an award this year, we think she’s totally deserving of a Best Dressed nod, for these two seriously sexy looks. Do you agree, HollywoodLifers?
