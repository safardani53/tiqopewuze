The Single Life' Cast Is Ready for Another Chance at Love

'90 Day: The Single Life' Season 3 cast is full of former cast members who all want another shot on reality TV, so who are they?

No matter what your feelings are about the cast of 90 Day: The Single Life, it's hard not to admit that we all love the mess they bring to the franchise. So when the 90 Day Fiancé spinoff made the leap from Discovery Plus to TLC, it made total sense. And now, viewers want to know more about the 90 Day: The Single Life cast for Season 3.

Article continues below advertisement

It's no secret that most of the relationships on 90 Day Fiancé are ill-fated and uncomfortable from the start. Some of them are born out of a one-sided attraction, while others go through multiple breakups before the season is over. Usually, it's a big mess that fans eat up. And if you love messes, then you'll be living for what comes next with this spin-off.

The 90 Day: The Single Life Season 3 cast:

Natalie Mordovtseva

Natalie and Mike Youngquist got married in Season 8 of 90 Day Fiancé, but things were never quite solid with them. Although they aren't yet legally divorced, Mike and Natalie are separated. This means Natalie is free to explore other options in Season 3 of 90 Day: The Single Life. But, judging by the trailer, she might find her way back to Mike sooner rather than later.

Article continues below advertisement

Veronica Rodriguez

Technically, Veronica never appeared in a 90 Day Fiancé show as a main cast member. But, because she's 90 Day Fiancé: The Other Way star Tim Malcolm's ex, she earned her spot in The Single Life. The only problem is that Tim might be the worst wingman she could ever have as she tries to make a lasting connection with someone else.

Article continues below advertisement

Debbie Johnson

Debbie first appeared in Season 6 of 90 Day Fiancé when her son, Colt Johnson, invited his fiancé Larissa Santos Lima to live with them in Las Vegas. While that relationship didn't work out, Debbie returned in other shows in the franchise as Colt continued his search for love. And now, Debbie is here to find love of her own — even if it means deepening a rift with her son.

Article continues below advertisement

Tania Maduro

Tania and Syngin Colchester separated for good after they were both in 90 Day: The Single Life Season 2. But before that, they shared their story in Season 7 of 90 Day Fiancé. And, although Tania is having a hard time explaining to prospective suitors on The Single Life that she's mentally but not literally divorced from Syngin, she's determined to find love again.

Article continues below advertisement

Tiffany Franco

Tiffany and her ex, Ronald Smith, were in multiple shows across the 90 Day Fiancé franchise before Tiffany was cast in Season 3 of The Single Life. Ronald reportedly told In Touch that he filed for divorce from Tiffany and she has clearly already moved on. But having a toxic ex never makes things easy when you want to find a new partner.

Article continues below advertisement

Caesar Mack

Caesar was in an online relationship with a woman from Ukraine for years before she finally broke things off with him in Season 3 of 90 Day Fiancé: Before the 90 Days. But he still wants that Ukrainian bride and in The Single Life, Caesar travels around the world to work with a matchmaker to make that happen.

We can't help but root for some of these 90 Day: The Single Life cast members. For others, we are more than ready to say I told you so.
