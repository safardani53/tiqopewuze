Roundhouse Comedy Festival: Dates, line-up and how to get tickets

COMEDY fans will be spoilt rotten this summer when the Roundhouse London hosts it's first ever festival.

The inaugural festival - in association with leading live production company Berk's Nest - will take place at the iconic venue in Camden this August.

Here's everything you need to know.

When is The Roundhouse Comedy Festival?

The initial slate of 2023 programming for the first ever Roundhouse Comedy Festival has been announced, with events taking place between August 5 â€“ 23, 2023.

Who is on the line-up?

The opening gala on August 5 will include Lolly Adefope, Jordan Gray and Rhys James.

Meanwhile, the comedy gala on August 8 will be lead by Dylan Moran, and he will be joined by Nish Kumar, Sindhu Vee and Suzi Ruffell.

Katherine Ryan and Friends will take place on August 17, with her special guests to be announced in due course.

James Acaster will take to the stage with his Hecklers Welcome show from Saturday, August 19 to Tuesday August 22.

The festival closing gala will be hosted by Ed Gamble and will feature Fern Brady and Sophie Duker.

How to buy tickets

Tickets are on sale now and all tickets for Roundhouse Comedy Festival can be purchased at www.roundhouse.org.uk.
