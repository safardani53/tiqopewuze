GWEN Stefani has fueled rumors that she could be pregnant by hiding her stomach in a baggy hoodie for a new baking video.

The Voice coach swamped her frame in loose clothes while making festive gingerbread cookies at home.

Standing in her kitchen, Gwen said to the camera: "As we know, I have a holiday record called You Make Me Feel Like Christmas and I have a song that I wrote with Blake Shelton.

"In the song, we talk about sweet gingerbread and I'm about ready to try to make some."

The Hollaback Girl singer, 53, wore a baggy navy hoodie as she made the cookie mixture.

The mother-of-three then changed into a baggy red hoodie and sweatpants and took the finished cookies over to Blake to show them off.

GWEN'S BROOD

Gwen has three children with her ex-husband Gavin Rossdale - Kingston, 16, Zuma, 14, and Apollo, eight.

She met Gavin in 1995 when No Doubt and Bush were on tour together.
