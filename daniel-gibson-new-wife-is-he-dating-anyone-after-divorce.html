Is He Dating Anyone After Divorce?

Daniel Gibson having a new wife might be rumors floating around on the internet. Gibson is currently single, and the last time he was married, he was still playing in the NBA. 

The former Cleveland Cavaliers player, Daniel Gibson, was drafted by the NBA team in 2006 as the 42nd overall pick.

He had a successful collegiate career with the University of Texas at Austin and was part of the McDonald’s All-American team during high school.

During his short stay with the Cavaliers, Gibson played alongside LeBron James and Darnell Jackson. 2012-13 marked the last season Gibson played in the NBA, as he quit the sport due to various reasons.

One of the reasons he stated during an interview was his rocky relationship with his former wife, Keyshia Cole.

The former couple ended their relationship in 2014, and recently the multi-Grammy award nominee Cole’s biopic Keyshia Cole: This is My Story was released. 

Daniel Gibson New Wife: Is He Dating Anyone After Divorce?

Looking through her Instagram, Sinfoy also seems to be single and focused on growing her career. 

Daniel Gibson Ex-Wife, Keyshia Cole, And Their Kid

Daniel Gibson’s former wife, Keyshia Cole, is an R&B singer and a four-time Grammy-nominated artist. 

Keyshia Cole began her career at age 16 and was once friends with Tupac Shakur. She released her first single in 2004 and was dubbed a top prospect in the music industry from her first album, The Way It Is. 

And if her first album helped her make a breakthrough, her second album, Just Like You, skyrocketed her to success. She secured four Grammy nominations at the 2009 Grammy Awards for the album. 

Cole has released seven studio albums, one EP, and two mixtapes and remains an influential figure in R&B and soul music scene. She has also appeared in reality TV shows, with one of them surrounding her and Daniel’s life. 

Daniel And Keyshia Relationship 

Daniel met his former partner Keyshia Cole through his stylist. The two shared the same stylist who thought it would be a great idea for them to meet and hang out. 

It was around 2008/09, so there wasn’t anything like sliding into the DMs. Gibson had to email Cole twice to catch her attention. 

So, after the emails, the two exchanged their numbers and started speaking on the phone regularly. After three months of talking only on the phone, they finally met at Daniel’s game. 

In an interview, Keyshia described it as love at first sight, and the feeling was mutual for Daniel. Soon after dating for a while, the couple announced their engagement in 2010.

The former Cavaliers player went down on one knee on New Year’s Eve and popped the big question with a 10-carat diamond ring. 

Shortly after their engagement, Gibson and Cole became parents for the first time with Daniel Hiram Gibson Jr. He was born on March 2, 2010.

The couple tied the knot, a year later, in 2011. At first, they had their wedding in Las Vegas in May for their reality show Family First.

Then they had their wedding ceremony for the second time in Hawaii, which at the time Cole described as a dream wedding ceremony for her husband. 

Daniel Gibson And Keyshia Cole Divorce 

Though it might have sounded like a fairytale love story at first, Daniel and Keyshia’s love didn’t stand the test of time. 

Three years after their marriage, the couple went their separate ways. Adnd three years after their separation, Cole filed for divorce, citing irreconcilable differences.

It took another three years for their divorce case to settle down. During this time, Keyshia welcomed a baby boy with her former partner Niko Khale.

The couple had previously shown they had a non-conventional method for co-parenting. They appeared together for the VH1 show Love & Hip Hop: Hollywood the same year Cole filed for divorce. 

Their son Daniel Jr. has made appearances on his parents’ Instagram handle and recently celebrated turning 13.
