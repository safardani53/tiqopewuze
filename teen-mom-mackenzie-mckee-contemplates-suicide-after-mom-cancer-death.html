Teen Moms Mackenzie Mckee admits I hate being alive and contemplates suicide after moms

MACKENZIE Mckee contemplated suicide on this week’s Teen Mom OG after the devastating loss of her beloved mother Angie from cancer.

On this week’s episode, Mackenzie, 25, opened up to her husband Josh, 26, about her depression and admitted she will “never be the same.”

The mom of three continued that she has “little hope” she will get better after the loss.

She said on TMOG: “Even though I am trying, a lot is going on right now and I need help. Sometimes you don’t like when I cry out to you, but it is very hard to want to be alive right now.”

When Josh responded, “If you feel like you’re depressed, you can try talking to someone, she replied, “Something has got to happen because something bad is going to happen.”

He then shockingly said before storming out of the room: "You saying that you’re going to kill yourself? I’m not going to be a part of that.”

Mackenzie then cried to a producer: “This is the support I have from him. I’m not going to do anything to myself cause I love my kids so much, but I hate being alive.

"It’s so painful. I’ve tried to have this conversation with him every single day and he just walks away from me.”

When Mackenzie called him to tell him how much he hurt her by walking away, he responded that her contemplating suicide made him “mad,” as they have three kids, Gannon, Jaxie and Broncs.

When he returned home, she said: “I wasn’t saying I’m going to kill myself cause I’m not going to do that to my kids. I did not think her death was going to be half this hard.”

But when Josh ignored her and walked away again, she cried and screamed: “You hate me! Just be here for me. I feel like I’m so desperate for you to love me, but you don’t.”

He responded: “I don’t know what else to do, you know how I feel. I love you.”

Angie lost her battle to cancer in December at age 50.
