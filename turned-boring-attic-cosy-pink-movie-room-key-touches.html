I turned my boring attic into a cosy pink movie room with H&amp;M accessories &amp; people are desperate to

A WOMAN has revealed how she turned her dull attic space into a cosy pink movie room.

TikTok user @emilywelchstyle showed how the room had previously been a plain gaming room with a desk but became a stylish retreat thanks to a few tweaks.

The star feature of the room is now a large sofa that Emily said is “bigger than a queen-sized bed”. 

She also added shelves to the wall and lots of plants and pretty accessories - including a head-shaped planter from H&M -  to provide the snug feel. 

In her video, Emily showed how she added lots of books and a mirror to finish off the look.

Her husband’s gaming station didn’t get fully booted out, and she explained it is now at the far end of the room, behind the camera.

More on home transformations

Her video has racked up over 1.7million views, so has clearly been a hit with viewers. 

One person said: “Your aesthetic is my literal dream.”

Another said of the couch: “The way I would end up just sleeping there too lol.”

A third commented: “Perfection. I don’t want to make this awkward but I’m moving in.”
