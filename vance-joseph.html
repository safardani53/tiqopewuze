Who is Vance Joseph:

Vance Joseph is a famous Football Coach. He was born on September 30, 1972 and his birthplace is Marrero, LA. Vance is also well known as, Defensive-minded coach who was offered his first head coaching opportunity by Denver Broncos GM John Elway in 2017. He was the defensive coordinator for the Miami Dolphins for the season prior.

Vance is originated from United States. He took over the role of head coach for the Broncos after Gary Kubiak stepped down from the position due to health concerns.
