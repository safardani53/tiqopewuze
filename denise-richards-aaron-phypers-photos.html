Photos Of The Couple Hollywood Life

Denise Richards and Aaron Phypers were first spotted together in September 2017. He was in the midst of divorcing ‘Desperate Housewives’ star Nicolette Sheridan, so Aaron and Denise tried keeping their budding romance a secret from the public. However, it was hard to keep their relationship under wraps after Denise joined the cast of ‘The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills.’ Denise revealed that she’d be starring on the show in Aug. 2018. ‘I am so excited to join the cast of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills as I am a huge fan of the show,’ she said at the time, adding, ‘I am looking forward to spending time with all the ladies … and hopefully meeting Boy George!’ And a few weeks later, just days after his divorce was finalized, Denise and Aaron tied the knot in Sept. 2018. Their wedding aired during Season 9 of the hit Bravo series. She was previously married to ‘Two and a Half Men’ star Charlie Sheen, whom she shares two daughters with. Denise also adopted a third daughter — as a single woman — in 2011, a few years after she split with Charlie.
