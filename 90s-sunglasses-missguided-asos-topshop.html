BIGGER isn't always better - especially when it comes to sunglasses.

Small shades are becoming an increasingly common sight, and it's all because of the nineties revival - and Kanye West.

In a recent episode of Keeping Up With The Kardashians, Kim explained how her fashion-loving rapper husband had sent her a very serious memo about ditching her usual big sunglasses for the smallest ones she could find.

She said: "Kanye sent me a whole email like, ‘You cannot wear big glasses any more. It’s all about tiny little glasses.'

“He sent me like, millions of 1990s photos with tiny little glasses like this."

Kim and Kanye aren't the only ones downsizing their sunglasses, with celebs from Rihanna to Bella Hadid spotted wearing the trend.

However, small glasses aren't for everyone, so perhaps now is the time to invest in a pair so you are used to the look before summer arrives.
