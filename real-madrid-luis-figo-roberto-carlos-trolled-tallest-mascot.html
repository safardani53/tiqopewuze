Luis Figo trolls Roberto Carlos as he makes pint-sized pal stand with tallest mascot in Real Madrid


LUIS FIGO made short work of Roberto Carlos in a game of giants - using a towering mascot to troll the pocket-sized Brazil icon.

The old pals were lining up for Real legends as they beat their Barcelona counterparts 3-2 in an exhibition clash.

Ronaldinho proved the star turn on the pitch - but it was Figo who gave it large in the tunnel.

Ex-Portugal hero Figo, 48, was one of six players in the special Tel Aviv showdown to have featured for both clubs during their distinguished careers.

But his sharpest action came before kick-off when he roped in the tallest mascot to stand next to 5ft 6ins icon Carlos, also 48.

The former left-back soon realised the prank - and roared with laughter.

But although the Real duo impressed out in the middle, they never quite reached the heights of Ronaldinho, a mere stripling at 41.

The former Barca wizard conjured up some of the weaves and jinks of his Nou Camp pomp, circa 2003-2008.

Ronaldinho's midfield team-mate Deco, plus opposite number Ivan Campo in the Real line-up, also gave classy throwback performances.

Ex-Bernabeu duo Pedro Munitis and Alfonso Perez overturned Ronaldinho's penalty opener, each netting within two second-half minutes.
