Angelina Pivarnick Marries Chris Larangeira!!! Join the Celebration!

They do, folks!

They really, truly, surely do – forever and ever.

Jersey Shore star Angelina Pivarnick married her fiancé, Chris Larangeira, on Wednesday!

The nuptials took place at the Park Château Estate & Gardens in East Brunswick, New Jersey, according to Entertainment Tonight.

The aforementioned celebrity gossip and news outlet broke this exciting piece of news and included with it a number of details related to Angelina’s big day.

To wit:

  • The blushing wore a strapless, white lace Castle Couture dress, which she teamed with a matching veil, silver tiara and jewelry.
  • She wore her hair down and accentuated it with hair extensions.
  • For his part, Larangeira looked as handsome as he could in a classic black tuxedo custom made by Karako Suits.

Many names familiar to fans of this franchise – Jenni “JWoww” Farley, Deena Cortese and Nicole “Snooki” Polizzi – served as Angelina’s bridesmaids.

Meanwhile, Paul “DJ Pauly D” DelVecchio, Vinny Guadagnino and Mike “The Situation” Sorrentino were all on hand to celebrate the nuptials.

However, Ronnie Ortiz-Magro and his on-off girlfriend, Jen Harley, did not attend, an insider has told Us Weekly.

Pivarnick has been with Larangeira for a little over two years.

The smoking hot couple got engaged in January of 2018, although a few questions arose over their future on this past season of Jersey Shore: Family Vacation.

How so?

In the main storyline from the second half of the story, Jenni’s then-boyfriend, Zach, got drunk and very clearly made moves on Pivarnick.

Zach later told Jenni that Angelina was actually the one who flirted with him and even kissed him, an allegation that Pivarnick screamed about at one point on a Jersey Shore episode.

She said it was putting her engagement in danger.

That may or may have not been true at the time, but it looks like Angelina and Chris came through that scandal unscathed in the end.

(And Jenni quickly kicked Zach to the curb after she witnessed on air how he truly behaved around Angelina several months ago.)

Speaking to People Magazine in May 2018, Pivarnick gushed about her relationship with Pivarnick.

“This is my third engagement. Third time’s a charm!” she told the publication back then, adding:

“I did not think I was going to get engaged again after my last engagement. It was toxic and it was terrible.”

“But last summer, I went out to some big pool party in a backyard,” she adds.

“I had known Chris for 14 years and he says the reason he never hit on me was because I always had a boyfriend.”

Who said chivalry is dead?

“We both worked in the mall when we were younger, and he says he used to see me walk past with a different boyfriend every week.”

As for what enthralling news we may be hearing next?

“I am sooooo excited about becoming a wife and having kids,” Angelina previously told ET, teasing an impending pregnancy:

“We are going to work on [the kids] rather quick.”
