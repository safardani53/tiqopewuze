Giannina Maradona Biography

The daughter of Argentinean legendary footballer Diego Maradona, Giannina Maradona is an Argentinean celebrity. She is also recognized as the ex-wife of striker Sergio Agüero.

Giannina Maradona Early Life, Social Media, Age

Giannina Maradona is a native of Argentina born on 16th May 1989. She is the daughter of the former professional footballer Diego Armando Maradona and his wife Claudia Villafane.

Maradona was raised alongside her sister Dalma Nerea, who was born two years prior to her. Giannina is also popular as the ex-wife of Argentine professional footballer Sergio Aguero.

Giannina Maradona is a media sensation with more than 325,000 followers on the social networking site, Twitter. However, she always came to the spotlight because of her father and husband, though she does have her own identity.
