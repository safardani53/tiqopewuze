Photos Of Famous Momager With Kids &amp; Grandkids Hollywood Life

The Karjenner tribe is a huge family, led by the wonderful Kris Jenner! From their reality empire (‘The Kardashians’ and its predecessor ‘KUWTK’) to their expanding fashion & cosmetic business ventures, it’s clear that the KarJenners are here to stay.

Nothing showed off their power as a family quite like their bold appearance at the 2019 Met Gala at the Metropolitan Museum of art in New York City on May 6, 2019. While they regularly hit the expansive carpet at fashion’s definitive event, 2019 was one for the books.

‘Momager’ Kris rocked an amazing black pants look, with Kim slaying in a dramatically tight ‘wet look’ dress, designed to look like she was just coming out of the water. Supermodel Kendall wore a stunning feathered orange gown, with cosmetics maven Kylie complementing with a lilac-hued gown and long, purple hair.
