Sports Illustrated MLB Playoff Preview Issue Cover Girl!

Sports Illustrated favorite Kate Upton is front and center on the magazine’s cover again this week, but not in a bikini for once (sadly). She’s in a Braves jersey!

Kind of shameless and definitely a stretch.

Other than that, sharing a similar surname, and the fact that Justin and B.J. play in Atlanta and Kate has probably heard of Atlanta, that’s it for commonality.
