Money-saving grocery hacks

While food prices continue to rise, there are some tricks that may save you serious cash.

Other retailers like Trader Joe's, Aldi, or Costco tend to offer their brand along with other, more expensive name brands.

The store brands are typically cheaper and nearly identical in taste so it could be worth a try.

Coupons.com is also a great source for savings.

The site has digital and printable coupons offering discounts on everything from food and cleaning supplies to personal care items.

While the platform offers coupons in almost every shopping category, you can set the filters to only show food deals.
