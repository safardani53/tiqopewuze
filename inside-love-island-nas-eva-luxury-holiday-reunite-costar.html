Inside Love Island stars Eva and Nas's luxury 500 a night holiday as they reunite with show co-sta

LOVE Island stars Nas Majeed and Eva Zapico have jetted off in search of sunnier climes, on a romantic trip to Greece.

The reality stars, who fell for each other during their stint on Winter Love Island back in January 2020, have been documenting their trip on social media.

In one snap, Nas strips topless for a photo while enjoying the sun, and in another he could be seen exploring the Acropolis in Athens.

The couple looked happier than ever in snaps shared on Nas’ Instagram three years on from their Love Island stint, captioned: “With love from Greece.”

Nas and Eva posed for a sun-soaked selfie in one, and an eagle-eyed follower replied in the comments: "Do I spy an engagement ring?? Xx"

Another raved: "Engaged?? Very happy that you guys are still going strong. You definitely put the definition in 'Love' island."

In another photo, Eva looked incredible as she donned a white top and shorts to head for dinner with her beau.

They also joined forces with a Love Island co-star and shared photos of their reunion.

Luke Trotman, who was on the same series as Eva and Nas, could be seen partying with the couple in a fun video short in Mykonos.

“Immaculate vibes in Mykonos,” Nas penned, to which Luke replied: “Absolute vibes.”

The couple have been staying at the stunning Saint John Hotel while on their holiday, which boasts an infinity pool, spa, fine dining and stunning sea views.

The rooms with sea views come at a cost of £544-a-night, or £3,810 for the week, while garden rooms will cost slightly less.

The rooms feature a minibar, a balcony, ensuite bathrooms, and of course, sea views.

Eva has been sharing a peek inside the trip on her own social media channels too, from playing tennis to posing with the sea as her backdrop.

Nas previously joke Eva was "begging to marry him" when they left Love Island.

The cheeky reality star shared the news with fans on social media claiming she wanted him to put a ring on her finger.

Nas previousy opened up about how nervous he was bringing Eva back to the main villa from Casa Amor - and facing the consequences with Demi.

He said: "It is the most nerve-wracking thing. Even speaking about it now brings back memories of how nervous I was.

“It’s 100 per cent more nervewracking than entering the villa for the first time.

“There are so many different thoughts going on - you have made your decision but then it’s also about what has happened on the other side, what the other boys are doing, did the person I was with recouple?”
