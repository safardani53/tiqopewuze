12 Best Women's Work Bags 2021 | The Sun UK

A HELPFUL bag for work that organises everything, is essential for coping with a busy lifestyle.

The best women's work bags are ones that are roomy enough for all your essentials and will easily house your laptop, to make the commute less of a hassle.

Totes come in all shapes and sizes, but what we think makes a great work bag is one with plenty of pockets allowing you to keep things in order.

Another handy option is one you can wear comfortably over your shoulder or across your body, as it leaves your hands free for those all-important emails you want to finish on your way home on your phone.

If you’re hoping to trade your old battered bag in for a sleek new number, there are plenty out there to choose from, however big your budget.

    What is the best women's work bag?

    When deciding which is the best women's work bag for you, consider how you are going to be using it and what you need to store inside.

    There's no point investing in a sturdy, waterproof style that doesn't hold A4 folders or your laptop.

    We recommend opting for a backpack if you pack a lot to go to work, to even out the weight and ensure that you don't mess up your back or posture.

    Where to buy women's work bags

    As you can see from our roundup, women's work bags can be found in just about any fashion retailer that stocks bags.

    In 2021, office dress codes are really loose, so you can get away with any kind of bag most of the time. You just need one that is practical enough for your needs.

    How much are women's work bags?

    There are lots of cheaper designs around that are still smart enough for work, but if you're spending less than a tenner on your bag then it's likely that it won't last you through your whole career.

    It's much better to invest in a bag that is nearer the £100 mark to ensure that it doesn't break and is durable enough to accompany you to work for years to come.
