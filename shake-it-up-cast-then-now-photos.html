Shake It Up is really a Disney Channel classic. The show premiered in 2010 and it introduced us to two of entertainment’s most up-and-coming talents. Starring Bella Thorne and Zendaya, Shake It Up followed the adventures of CeCe Jones (Bella) and Rocky Blue (Zendaya) after they landed gigs as background dancers on a local show Shake It Up Chicago. It ran for three seasons, and there was even a feature-length special in Made In Japan in 2012.

It’s wild to think it’s been 12 years since the show’s start. Since the show premiered, the stars of Shake It Up have gone on to do huge things. Wonder where Zendaya, Bella and the rest are up to now? Check out our gallery here for more.
