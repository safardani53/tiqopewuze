Victoria Justice Hollywood Life

Bio:
Victoria Justice (born Victoria Dawn Justice in Hollywood, Florida on February 19, 1993) is an American singer-songwriter, actress, and dancer. She is best known for her role as Tori Vega on Nickelodeon’s Victorious. Her other Nickelodeon credits include Zoey 101, The Naked Brothers Band, iCarly, and True Jackson, VP. She also appeared in the television movies Spectacular! and The Boy Who Cried Werewolf. Her hit songs, including “Best Friend’s Brother,” “Make It Shine,” and “Freak The Freak Out” appear on the soundtracks for Victorious and Spectacular! Her solo debut album is due out in late 2014. She also appeared in the film Fun Size in 2012, alongside Johnny Knoxville and Chelsea Handler.

Personal Life:
Victoria started dating actor Ryan Rottman since early 2011. Shortly after they began dating, Rottman was arrested for a DUI. In Dec. 2013, Victoria and Ryan broke up, and she started dating Pierson Fode. She was previously linked to Nicholas Hoult and Dillon Moore. She has shot down rumored involvement with Taylor Lautner and Victorious costar, Avan Jogia.
