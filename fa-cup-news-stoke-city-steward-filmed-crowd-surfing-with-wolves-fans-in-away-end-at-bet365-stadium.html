Stoke City steward filmed crowd surfing with Wolves fans in FA Cup clash at bet365 stadium

A STEWARD at Stoke City was filmed caught crowd surfing with Wolverhampton Wanderers fans in the away end during the Championship side's FA Cup victory over Stoke on Saturday.

The clip was filmed and uploaded by a Wolves supporting Twitter, and comes just days after another steward was caught singing with Manchester United fans in the away end at West Ham.

The Hammers did an investigation and found that steward was a Manchester United employed one - and Jon Foster, who filmed the Wolves version claims he is a Wolves fan.

Nevertheless, the steward bounces up and down in the air wearing his yellow fluorescent jacket as fans sing the club anthem "Wolves Ay We".
