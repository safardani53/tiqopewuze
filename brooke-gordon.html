Brooke Gordon Biography

Brooke Gordon is a former Miss Winston Cup model who greeted drivers in the victory lane of racing. Gordon is widely known for being the ex-spouse of stock car racer, Jeff Gordon.

Early Life of Brooke Gordon

Brooke Sealey, or Brooke Gordon, was born in the city of Winston, in the United States. She has not given any further details regarding her date of birth and of her parents. She is of American nationality and her ethnicity is white. Her educational details is also a mystery.

Brooke used to work as one of those girls who greet the racers in the racing tracks. She rose to fame when she became Miss Winston. During her time in the racing tracks, she met future husband Jeff Gordon, which ultimately had her taste fame.

Brooke Gordon is a married woman and has been married twice. Jeff Gordon met Brooke in and was married in . The proposal was made by Jeff at the 1994 Daytona 500 in February. The couple didn't have any kids of their own, and after being married for 8 years, she filed for a divorce, which went down in the history of sports as one of the most expensive divorces.
