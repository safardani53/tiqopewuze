Donald Trump Jr. and Kimberly Guilfoyle: ALREADY Getting Engaged?!

Believe it or not, Kimberly Guilfoyle and Donald Trump Jr. are dating. People on both sides of the aisle agree that they deserve each other, but each side says that with very different meaning.

Guilfoyle has already signed on to work to get Trump reelected, quitting her job at Fox News where she was doing the exact same thing.

But in a brand new photo that she shared, she stands with Don Jr. while wearing a conspicuously large ring. Are … are they engaged?

Back in March, Vanessa Trump filed for divorce from Don Jr. after weeks of speculation and eye-opening reports that their marriage was on its last leg.

A couple of months later, Don Jr. starts dating Kimberly Guilfoyle, which actually makes a lot of sense.

Female Fox News hosts seem to be required to be attractive — a requirement that does not extend to their male colleagues — and, of course, aggressively conservative.

Guilfoyle is both, making her a great match.

And it looks like Don Jr. was a perfect partner for her, too — and good for her! Not everybody wants a partner who’s smart or handsome or law-abiding.

But … if they’re just dating, what is she doing with this huge ring?

"A winning team!" Guilfoyle declared in the captions. "I was thrilled to join @donaldjtrumpjr yesterday at #hsls2018 @turningpointusa!"

The two attended some nightmarish conference for young conservatives, but that’s not what caught people’s attention.

No, Kimberly was sporting a sizable ring on her left hand.

It’s not just a big ring — it’s a big diamond ring.

A report says that these two are preparing to become engaged.

RadarOnline reports that Don Jr. and Kimberly Guilfoyle will become engaged just as soon as his divorce from his wife is finalized.

"She is completely smitten with Don," a source tells the tabloid.

That’s not something that he hears everyday!

But Guilfoyle is swept up in their relationship, the insider claims, "and can’t stop telling people how happy he makes her."

That’s no surprise — he just got her a fancy new job as Vice Chairwoman of his dad’s campaign!

We’re sure that Trump donors are thrilled to hear that Don Jr. is using their donations to keep getting laid.

And speaking of love …

"And he has already told her he loves her," a source reveals.

He’s thoroughly besotted, the insider continues, "and wants to be with her for the rest of his life."

‘Til prison do them part! It’s so romantic.

They say that fools rush in, but isn’t getting engaged to your rebound right after your divorce kind of pushing it?

"They know people say it’s too soon and have been passing comment about their relationship," the source acknowledges. "But they don’t care."

Well, if the Trump family cared about public opinion, a lot of things would be different.

"They want to get engaged right away," the insider dishes. "And are only waiting for his divorce from Vanessa to come through."

Well, they can do what they like with their lives, we suppose.

And, for the record, even Vanessa Trump has defended the fact that they’re dating. Though … we do wonder how she’ll feel about such a speedy engagement.
