Gino and Jasmine almost break up on 90 Day Fiance after she finds suspicious item Sun 12 Novemb

Gino and Jasmine are on the verge of break up after she found a suspicious item in his car. When she asks her 90 Day Fiance: Before The 90 Days partner what it is, Jasmine claims he’s acting “guilty”. Gino Palazzolo responds by saying the lip gloss could be a co-worker’s, much to his partner’s shock.

The TLC couple have had several fall-outs during their relationship. 90 Day Fiance duo Gino is accused of cheating by his partner Jasmine after she finds a shiny lip gloss on the passenger seat floor of his car. He denies even knowing what the item is, but claims he hasn’t gone out with a co-worker.

Gino and Jasmine almost break up

The two are facing a break up on 90 Day Fiance, after Jasmine finds a lip gloss in his car that wasn’t hers. She shouts at Gino, “Who is this?” to which he replies: “I don’t know.”

He then adds: “It could be one of my co-workers’.” To which Jasmine replies: “Wait wait wait, you go out with co-workers?” However, Gino denies this… Yet Jasmine is not convinced.

She says in a confessional: “The way he’s responding to me, I can tell he’s guilty.” She ends up accusing Gino of cheating on her with a mystery woman, before she cries, and wants to go home.

90 Day Fiance star accuses Gino of cheating

Jasmine accused Gino of cheating on her after finding a lip gloss in his car. He strongly denies going out with co-workers or even knowing what the lip gloss is, but Jasmine thinks he is “guilty” by his reaction.

“I’ve never been with a girl since I’ve been with you,” Gino tells Jasmine. She calls him a “disgusting person” while Gino continues driving. He tells the cameras that Jasmine is screaming like there’s “murder”.

“I wanna go back to Panama,” Jasmine continues howling until she makes Gino stop the car. She tells Gino he is a cheater and says she doesn’t want to see him ever again. “You’re crazy,” Gino tells Jasmine.

Jasmine wants to pick up her dog and belongings from Gino’s house before he can call an Uber for her to go to a hotel. Beforehand, she wasn’t impressed by Gino’s house, which she claimed smelled of garbage.

Where Gino and Jasmine are now

Gino hasn’t been featured on Jasmine’s Instagram page for months. Even when he has, she states the post is “old” or was taken in 2022. However, she made an appearance on Gino’s page on October 15.

Jasmine moved to Michigan to live with him but found a lip gloss on his car floor on the same day. Gino quit his job while Jasmine complained about the weather and location immediately upon arrival.

Gino is believed to have moved to Miami. At the beginning of October, the couple posed for a cozy snap with the rest of the 90 Day Fiance cast, where Jasmine has her hand on Gino’s leg.
