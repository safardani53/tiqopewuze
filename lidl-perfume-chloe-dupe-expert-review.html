Im a perfume expert and tested out Lidls 4 dupe of a Chloe scent and heres my honest revi

A FRAGRANCE expert has shared his honest thoughs on a Lidl perfume that is claimed to be a dupe of Chloe.

Thomas took to social media to share his thoughts on the dupe and whether it was worth searching in your local Lidl for.

He said: "This is Chalou from Lidl, I paid 35 for this 75ml bottle.

This is a pretty damn good dupe of Chloe, the eau de parfum, let's talk about it"

He said if you were after a floral, light femine scent to wear every day, much like the Chloe perfume, then you can't go wrong with the Lidl fragrance.

Describing the scent, Thomas said: "It opens with this aggressivley feminin floral pink bouquet."

Notes include sweet peas, honeysuckle, magnolia and musk.

The fragrance pro added that if he didn't already know it was the dupe from Lidl, he wouldn't be able to tell it wasn't the orginal.

"Good fragrance can cost you an absolute fortune," he added.

"Similarly, good fragrance that you can throw in your handbag, throw in your cluthc, take for a night out, can also cost £5 and you can buy it from Lidl."
