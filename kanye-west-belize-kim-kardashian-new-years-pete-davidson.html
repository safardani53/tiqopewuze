Kanye West touches down in Belize- as ex Kim Kardashian 'plans to spend New Year's Eve with Pete Dav

AS Kim Kardashian prepares to ring in the new year in Miami with new boyfriend Pete Davidson, her ex Kanye West is spending time in an entirely different tropical environment.

The rapper landed in Belize on Wednesday, along with friend and collaborator J Prince.

The music producer and promoter owns an island off the Central American coast, but according to TMZ, it's not clear if Kanye was there for pleasure, business or both.

Meanwhile, ex Kim is getting ready for some pleasure after Pete finishes his business of hosting a TV special welcoming in the new year in Miami.

The 28-year-old comedian is currently prepping for host a New Year’s Eve special with Miley Cyrus in South Florida, and Kim plans to be his special "plus one."

After noting that both Pete’s personal and professional life are “going amazing,” A source told HollywoodLife: “As New Year’s comes closer, he has already asked for Kim to join him in Miami, and it is not out of the realm of possibility that they may appear together as the ball drops on the special.

“He’s leaving it all in her hands on if she wants to join or not, the invite has been made.”

One thing sure to be missing from the special: Pete's long hair.

Just days before the show, Miley shared a photo of Pete revealing he shaved his platinum blond locks to return to his signature buzz cut look with his natural dark hair.

The comedian held a mic and smiled next to Miley as she sang.

Pete was wearing a brown hooded sweatshirt with light grey sweatpants and sliders.

Miley, 29, sported zebra print leggings paired with a red blazer.

The pop singer captioned her post: "He showed up," tagging the New Year's Eve hashtag, Pete Davidson, and NBC.

HEATING UP

Kim's invite to the TV special taping comes as her romance with Pete continues to heat up.

The Keeping Up With the Kardashians alum was said to have met up with the comic at the Beverly Hills Hotel in Los Angeles for a lunch date over the weekend.

The famous pair were spotted cozying up at the Polo Lounge in the esteemed hotel.

The SKIMS mogul appeared to go makeup-free for the casual meet up, while Pete wore a grey beanie cap with a black hooded sweatshirt.

According to Hollywood Life, Pete had his arm wrapped around Kim and gazed into her eyes as they appeared deep in conversation.

BICOSTAL ROMANCE

Earlier this month, the stars were also spotted at a Staten Island movie theatre as they were photographed walking through Atrium Stadium Cinemas lobby.

Things have become so serious that the mom of four has even met Pete's family.

According to In Touch, the he "introduced" Kim to his mom, Amy, and his younger sibling, Casey, as "they all met up on Staten Island."

The outlet revealed that Amy as well as Casey gave "their seal of approval."

NOT GIVING UP

But even as Kim and Pete have grown closer, ex-husband Kanye, 44, is still holding out hope for a reunion of his own.

The rapper has reportedly "bought a $4.5M mansion across the street from Kim's $60M estate" with plans to tear it down and rebuild.

"Kanye's new house is a teardown. He only bought it for the location," a source told PEOPLE. "It's near Kim and the kids. He has a vision for the house and plans on starting construction ASAP.

"It will be family-friends. He wants to be able to have the kids over as much as possible. Living only in Malibu was making visits more tricky for Kanye."
