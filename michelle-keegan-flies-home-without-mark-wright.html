Michelle Keegan looks tense as she flies home from Spain without husband Mark Wright

MICHELLE Keegan looked gutted to be heading home from Spain early after her best pal’s wedding this weekend.

The actress, 36, was spotted landing back in Manchester following a few days in the sun partying with her friends and husband Mark Wright.

Mark stayed on in Spain to celebrate another friend’s wedding and was seen at the star studded nuptials of Kenneth Shepherd to Lucy Wright at the Cathedral of Palma, Mallorca.

Just a couple of days before Michelle and Mark had celebrated her best friend Rachel’s big day.

The pair were seen partying on a boat in the lead up to the wedding enjoying some well earned time off work.

They even shared a rare picture together before the ceremony, sending their fans wild.

The couple have been happily married for eight years, but due to their intense work schedules, they have had to endure a long-distance relationship at times.

Recently Mark filmed his new BBC show One's A Wright Family Holiday, across the UK, while Michelle spent months in Australia working on BBC drama Ten Pound Poms.

Despite being very busy, the couple do still manage to spend quality time with one another, but like to keep it private.

Speaking about their relationship to the MailOnline, Mark said: "Do you know what, whenever there's a picture of us together people will say 'rare picture of Mark and Michelle' or 'Mark and Michelle reunite after time apart'.
