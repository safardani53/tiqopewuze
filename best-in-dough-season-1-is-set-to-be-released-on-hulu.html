Best In Dough Season 1 Is Set To Be Released On Hulu

Best in Dough brings together pizza-obsessed participants from all walks of life to compete in crafting unusual flavored pizzas and pizza styles for a chance to win $10,000. The first three episodes of Best in Dough will be available on Hulu on Monday, September 19 at 12:01 a.m. ET and the next three episodes will be available on the same platform the following Monday, September 26. The last four episodes will be available on Hulu on October 3 at 12:01 a.m. ET.

Adam Wells, a former Bachelorette candidate, and pizza enthusiast will host the episode. Chef Daniele Uditi will serve as the show’s head chef, while judges will include chef Millie Peartree, culinary influencer Eunji Kim, and baker Bryan Ford.

Best in Dough competitions, pizza-making challenges, and more

Each episode of Finest in Dough will have three contestants with similar backgrounds, careers, or ages trying to bake the best pizzas in order to win $10,000 and the title of Best in Dough. Adam Wells, the presenter, will put the participants through two fierce challenge rounds. The tasks, as shown in the teaser, may range from using no dough to producing pizza or pizza cupcakes. Each task will push participants to produce mind-blowing pizza mash-ups with a range of tastes.

The first three episodes of Best in Dough, which will premiere on September 19, will put Nonnas (Italian grandmothers), social media influencers, and pitmasters against one another. The next three episodes will showcase fierce pizza-making battles between local pizza pop-ups in the United States, world champion pizza chefs, and college students.

Three mother-daughter teams, three chefs from Mexico, Japan, and Brazil, three chefs from the Midwest (Chicago, Cleveland, and Detroit), and three fine dining chefs will compete to produce the finest pizza in the show’s final four episodes.

Who is Wells Adams, the host of Best in Dough?

Wells Adams, a professional radio DJ from Monterey, California, was born in 1984. Wells was on The Bachelorette Season 12 but was removed following his one-on-one date with Joelle Fletcher in week six. He later participated in Season 3 of Bachelor in Paradise but was removed following his divorce from Ashley Iaconetti. In the fourth season, he took over for Jorge Moreno as the hotel bartender, a post he held for four seasons.

Since 2017, Wells Adams has been dating Modern Family actress Sarah Hyland. The pair proposed in 2019 and intended to marry in August 2020. However, because of the covid-19 epidemic, they postponed their plans and eventually married on August 20, 2022.

Wells told People in an interview:

“I can honestly say that I’ve never had so much fun or acquired so much weight as I did while working on Best in Dough. Hosting was an absolutely wonderful job! In a world where no one can agree on anything, we can all agree on one thing: pizza is the finest, and this program is a giant slice of paradise.”

Adams has also been on GMA3: Strahan, Sara, and Keke, as well as The Kendall K. & Friends Show. He hosts the podcast ‘Your Favorite Thing with Wells & Brandi,’ which he co-hosts with Brandi Cyrus.
