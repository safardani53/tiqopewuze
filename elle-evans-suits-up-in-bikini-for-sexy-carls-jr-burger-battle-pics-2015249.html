Elle Evans Suits Up in Bikini for Sexy Carl's Jr. Burger Battle: Pics

Now that’s sizzlin’! Elle Evans has donned a patriotic bikini to stand by the ladies of Team U.S.A. in a volleyball game against Team Mexico in a commercial for Carl’s Jr.’s new burger, and Us Weekly has the exclusive first look at the model all suited up for action.

The model and actress, known for her appearance in Robin Thicke’s “Blurred Lines” video, shows off her lean and toned body in a red, white, and blue bikini in the photo, while holding an equally U.S.-themed volleyball and, of course, a giant, juicy burger.

The burger at the center of attention is the fast-food chain’s new Tex Mex Bacon Thickburger, featuring an Angus beef patty, poblano peppers, applewood smoked bacon, Sante Fe Sauce, pepper Jack cheese, and all the classic burger fixings. 

As for that volleyball game? It’s part of the “Borderball” commercial — launching Sept. 28 – in which Evans, 25, and her team will face off against model Alejandra Guilmant and a few other ladies from Mexico to determine one very important question: Is the burger more Tex, or is it more Mex?
