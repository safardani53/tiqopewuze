Izabel Goulart Wiki: From Sensual Romance With Boyfriend To Family, Diet &amp; Workout

Brazilian Model Izabel Goulart's gorgeous figure attracted two footballers back to back. She first dated Brazilian footballer and then started dating German footballer since 2015. 

The São Carlos native is best known as a former Victoria's Secret Angel, associated from 2005 to 2016. The fashion model is associated with an American magazine, Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue and Italian fashion house, Armani Exchange.

Controversial Start Of The Career: Izabel Goulart's Wiki

The daring model was born on 23 October 1984 in a family of  Italian and Portuguese descent. She has five siblings; four brothers and a sister. Izabel loves her family and enjoys spending quality time with them.

She is the number one fan of her father and regards her mother as the best mother in the world being her best friend. 

The 33-year-old had traits of a successful model during her school days. Blessed with a height of 5 feet 10 inches, slender figure and a photogenic face, she was suggested by a hairstylist to become a model, when she was just 14. Izabel joined the modeling agency, Success in São Paulo in 2001. 

However, the start of her career did not go as planned. She experienced a wardrobe malfunction on her first appearance in a fashion show. Despite the setback,  she continued to walk the runway and featured in collections made by various A-list designers.

In 2005, her career took a massive leap after joining Victoria's Secret as an Angel. During her stint with the women's fashion brand for nearly 11 years, she walked in the runway of  Victoria's Secret Fashion Show numerous times.

After leaving Victoria's Secret in 2016, she became an independent fashion model. She has since been featured in several high-profile magazines such as Vogue, GQ, and Glamour.

The supermodel boasts an 'abtastic' body and a slender figure, which has seen her become one of the most wanted-after names in the world of fashion for nearly two decades.

Izabel shared the secret of her slender frame to Vogue in January 2017 and said she did not prefer extreme and crash dieting and believed in low-calorie life. She started her day with a fresh juice and preferred a protein drink after every workout.

Furthermore, she deemed health as one of the most significant treasures we have in life, and it had always been her priority. She also suggested a healthy diet and regular workout as the reason for her success in maintaining her body.

Not only has her beauty been featured in the covers of magazines and landed her a fat modeling contract, but it has also left some footballers swooning. 

The charming lady has attracted big-name footballers in her dating life. She was in a relationship with a Brazilian footballer, Marcelo Costa, 37. However, their romance endured only for a few years. 

After Marcelo, she is now dating a German footballer boyfriend, Kevin Trapp enjoying some romantic getaways together.
