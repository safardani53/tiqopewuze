Daredevil filmed standing up straight on 'sticky wall' ride while it spins at high speeds at Ayrshir

A DAREDEVIL has been filmed standing up on a fairground gravity wheel ride as it spins at high speed.

The popular ride - also known as a 'sticky wall' - uses centrifugal force to pin thrill-seekers to the wall, against which they are supposed to lie completely flat.

But a jaw-dropping clip filmed at a festival in Scotland shows a man standing on the wall with his head towards the centre of the rapidly-spinning ride.

The clip from the Irvine Marymass festival, Ayrshire, has now gone viral.
