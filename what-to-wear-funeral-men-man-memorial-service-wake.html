What to Wear to a Funeral (for Men) in 2022 | The Sun UK

WONDERING what to wear to a funeral? When choosing menswear, consider the type of service it is and the wishes of those who have organised it.

A funeral outfit can be tricky for men but would usually involve a black suit and tie with a white shirt, but at a memorial service, this may be seen as too sombre for the occasion.

Similarly, some families may request a more casual approach to dressing, or even that people wear bright, lively clothes in celebration of the deceased’s life: there are many ways to approach such a difficult time.

The important thing is always to be respectful - making sure that your clothes are appropriate, and that they are clean, pressed and polished.

If in any doubt at all, it is always best to ask those organising the funeral or memorial for their advice while you offer your condolences.

And remember, it is better to be overdressed than underdressed.

With this in mind, discover our top picks below for menswear suitable for a funeral.

What to wear to a traditional funeral

Where the dress code has not been specified, it is safe to assume that traditional funeral attire would be appropriate. In the UK, this consists simply of a suit in black or dark grey with a white shirt and dark tie, preferably in black.

A black shirt is also appropriate as well as white, but you should always wear black shoes above any colour.

Putting some effort into presentation is considered respectful. Ensure that your shirt is well ironed and your shoes freshly polished. If wearing cufflinks or jewellery, keep them smart and understated.

Remember that in winter you may need to wear a coat as well. Again, wearing something smart and in a sombre colour will be the most appropriate option.

What to wear to a more relaxed funeral

Some people specify that they do not want their funerals to be so formal, or their families may make the same request of guests.

In this case, it is still best to keep your outfit relatively smart and subdued — but you can opt to not wear a suit here. For example, wear a crew neck jumper with chinos or tailored trousers and smart shoes. Choose dark colours to remain respectful while also feeling more at ease.

What to wear to a memorial service

Memorial services usually take place some time after a person has passed away, and serve as a celebration of that person’s life and a space to remember them. In this case, the mood is usually less sombre.

It is still considered respectful to dress smartly, but it is not necessary to be totally formal, and it’s fine to wear colours and patterns.

A navy suit would be an appropriate option, or a blazer and chinos for a more casual look. Try a plain shirt with a patterned tie or a striped shirt with a plain tie.

While brighter colours are fine, this is not the time to experiment with bold tones or clashes — keep things fairly simple for a respectful look.
