Meghan Markle Wears Personalized Necklace for Prince Harry: Pics

Royally adorable! While Prince Harry and Meghan Markle have yet to step out together publicly, the Suits actress is making their relationship status known with one sweet accessory.

Markle, 35, went shopping in Toronto Saturday, December 3, wearing a blush wrap coat, distressed skinny jeans, a Marc Jacobs clutch and a notable piece of jewelry: a dainty necklace personalized with the letters M and H.

Specifically, she wore the Maya Brenner Asymmetrical Letter & Charm Necklace, a delicate piece available in white, yellow or rose gold starting at $240. (Each letter beyond the first is an additional $60.)

And she isn’t the only one smitten. Though he was set to return home from his tour of the Caribbean Sunday, December 4, Prince Harry jetted to Toronto to visit Markle, squeezing in a quick trip before he’s expected back in London Wednesday, December 7.

Now that the prince has completed his tour on behalf of his grandmother, Queen Elizabeth II, and Markle has wrapped the sixth season of Suits, the two should be able to spend more time together — which is exactly what they plan to do. As Us Weekly previously reported, the pair are planning a romantic getaway. “It will be a one-week trip,” a source told Us. “There are just a few details left to finalize.”
