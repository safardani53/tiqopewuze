Geordie Shores Sophie Kasaei poses with rarely-seen lookalike family on night out

GEORDIE Shore personality Sophie Kasaei posed with her lookalike family on a night out.

The Kasaei family are known in the South Shields area for owning the Mambo's restaurant group.
