Did Ashton Kutcher Cheat on Demi Moore?

They may have been scoffed at when they first got together, due to their age difference, but Ashton Kutcher and Demi Moore have proven to be one of the happiest, most stable couples in Hollywood.

Or so we thought! A new tabloid report has cast a shadow on this relationship, as the latest issue of Star Magazine claims Ashton has been caught cheating on his wife.

Ashton Kutcher Tweets a lot. For the sake of his wife, let’s hope those are the only messages he sends out to strangers… if you catch our drift!

Of a recent incident at the Los Angeles Italian eatery Madeo, a witness tells the magazine:

“Ashton had this gorgeous girl pinned against the wall, and he was totally making out with her. I was shocked to see him sucking face with a girl who obviously wasn’t Demi.”

 Kutcher’s rep has denied the report, while a friend of Moore’s says the actress will be “devastated” if it’s true.

We say: look, the guy has a huge penis. Can you blame him for wanting to show it off?!?

* UPDATE: Ashton has responded to this story with the following Tweet: “I think Star magazine calling me a “cheater” qualifies as defamation of character. I hope my lawyer agrees.”
