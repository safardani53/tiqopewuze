Chrissy Teigen, More Stars Reveal Favorite Halloween Candies: Photos

Celebrities are just like Us, and they have a favorite go-to Halloween candy that they indulge in every holiday season.

While Kristen Bell exclusively told Us Weekly that she loves “anything with peanut butter,” Ariel Winter and Emmy Rossum chose the controversial candy corn as their top Halloween treat.

Winter called the Jelly Belly Candy Company’s multi-colored confection “so delicious,” complaining that it “kills [her]” that you can’t get it in-store every week. Rossum, meanwhile, exclusively told Us that she holds out for candy corn and “only ever [eats] it on Halloween!”

Bethenny Frankel, for her part, told Us she thinks Dark Chocolate Snickers is the “best candy” and would hand them out to Halloween revelers if it were not for concerns about kids with peanut allergies.
