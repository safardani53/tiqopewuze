What was Sonia Pizarro's cause of death?

Pizarro's niece, Lyndah Pizarro, confirmed her aunt's death to TMZ on May 4, 2023, revealing that she had died the night prior in her Arizona home.

Despite confirming her death, Lyndah did not provide a cause, however, she told the outlet that she had a history of serious health issues.

In 2018, Pizarro was hospitalized after suffering a stroke, but that is not thought to have contributed to her sudden death.

After her death was announced, Tercero posted a tribute on Instagram, remembering his ex as a "bad a** chick."
