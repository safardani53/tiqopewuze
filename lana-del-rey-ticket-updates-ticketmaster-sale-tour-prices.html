Promo and discount codes

Lana Del Rey's ticket concerts are expected to be expensive, but there are a few codes you can use to save money.

These were collected by Rolling Stone.

If using VividSeats you can use the code RS2023 to save $20.

If using Seatgeek, you can use the code SEATGEEK10 to save $10.

Presale is live NOW

Visit Ticketmaster.com to get your Lana del Rey tickets as soon as possible!

Tips to look to before general sale

Ticketmaster has released a set of tips to guide fans who are purchasing tickets through the process.

They recommend:

  • Sign in or create a Ticketmaster account. Have your password handy or reset it in advance to avoid any delays.
  • Ensure your billing and delivery details are up to date.
  • Have a back-up plan – if the show you wanted sells out, have a second venue or date in mind to book
  • Double-check your time zones to make sure you’re selecting your desired event and venue.
  • Download the Ticketmaster app so you can manage your tickets from your device.

What to do if tickets sell out

If tickets sell out, you can buy them online through websites such as VividSeats or StubHub.

Ticketmaster’s resell service also allows you to purchase resold tickets.

Simply search for the band or event you wish to attend and check if resold tickets are available.

Contacting Ticketmaster about issues

On their website, Ticketmaster offers phone and email contact options.

The website has a chat icon that you can use to engage in live chat with a customer service agent.

You’ll need to check into your account before you can access a representative 24/7 by clicking the Live Chat option under the order details of your ticket.

A representative can be reached by phone at (800) 653-8000 for alternative contact information.

When you phone customer support, don’t forget to be prepared to provide your order number.

From 9am to 9pm ET, Monday through Sunday, they offer customer service.

How to check if Ticketmaster is down

In cases where the site is slow to update with complaints, users might check Twitter to see if anyone else is experiencing a similar problem.

The number of complaints they’ve received about the app will be reflected on another website, Service Provider, and comments are displayed at the bottom of the page.
