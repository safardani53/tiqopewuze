Peacocks is selling a version of these glittery Gucci trainers and they're 420 cheaper

STEP out in all that glitters with these designer dupe trainers from Peacocks.

The budget fashion store is selling these fun lace-up pumps which appear to be inspired by a pair from Gucci.

Pink glitter trainers, £20, Peacocks

Like the shoes produced by the Italian luxury brand, the Peacocks pair are completely covered in sparkling glitter.

But unlike the designer sneakers, which cost an eye-watering £450, the high street version will set you back just £20.

The main visual difference is that the Gucci trainers feature a metallic stripe on the side, and a plain white sole.

The all-over glitter Peacocks pumps come in baby pink or a blue-green shade and are available to buy in stores now, or on the retailer’s website.
