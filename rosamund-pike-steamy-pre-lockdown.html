Rosamund Pike looks hot in lingerie after starring in Thomas the TankEngines 75th anniversary

ROSAMUND Pike looks hot on one’s (train) tracks as she stuns in a photo-shoot taken before lockdown.

The Gone Girl actress, 41, is set to star in Thomas the Tank Engine’s 75th anniversary episode where she will voice the role of the Duchess of Loughborough, who ‘feels the pressure.’

The special show, which airs on UK television this Saturday, is entitled Thomas & Friends: The Royal Engine, and will celebrate the heritage of the brand and also feature an on-screen introduction by Harry, The Duke of Sussex, 35.

Her Majesty The Queen and HRH, The Prince of Wales, when he was just a boy, are also characters in the programme, which sees the famous train visiting the city of London to receive an honor.

Speaking about her appearance, the star admitted that she had previously met The Queen before, saying: “I met the Queen at the premier of my first film which was a Bond film called Die Another Day, as we had a Royal Premiere at The Royal Albert Hall.

“I practiced my curtsy, saying ma’am and when to say, “Your Royal Highness” or “Your Majesty.”
