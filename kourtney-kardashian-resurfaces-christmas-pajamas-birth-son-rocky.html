Kourtney Kardashian resurfaces on social media in her Christmas pajamas just weeks after giving birt

KOURTNEY Kardashian has resurfaced on social media in her pajamas ahead of Christmas Day.

The Poosh account shared an image of its founder in an Instagram Story. 

The snapshot featured The Kardashians star playing around with her long dark hair.  

Kourtney, 44, donned red and white festive pajamas with drawings of reindeer on the front.

The Hulu star pouted her lips as she grabbed chunks of her messy chair in each hand. 

The caption shared a link with tips on how to sleep better during the holidays.

Kourtney originally posted the photo back in December 2018, according to E! Online.

'TAKING IT EASY'

The post came after the Lemme founder explained her online absence.

On Wednesday, the reality star shared a black-and-white workout video of herself exercising on a treadmill.

She pointed the camera at her legs as she marched on the machine.

Kourtney explained: "Seven weeks postpartum: first day in the gym doing 30 minutes walking 3.0 12.0 incline.

"Taking it easy, no rush, no pressure mamas."

She closed with: "Your body is healing. It's not a race."

NOT INVITED?

The former E! star has not been seen in public, nor on social media, since giving birth to baby Rocky Thirteen Barker at the start of November.

Reports have claimed Kourtney and her husband, Travis Barker, 47, kept Rocky in hiding and away from family to enjoy time with him alone.

A source close to the family said that the Barker/Kardashian clan has refused family visits.

The TV personality has kept a strict invite-only rule for anyone heading out to visit her baby boy, The U.S. Sun exclusively learned in early November.

The insider told The U.S. Sun: "Kourtney and Travis have communicated that they will be taking 'family only' time for the newly blended family and for Kourt to rest, and then they will be inviting the extended family over on an individual basis."

According to the insider, even Kourtney's younger sister Kim Kardashian, 43, "still hasn't met" her new nephew.

Another source added: "Kourtney always wanted 40 days to recuperate after giving birth.

"Her pregnancy was hard and physically and emotionally challenging, and she had no intention of rushing back out there - she wants to slow it all down.

"She says she has nothing to prove but wanted to make sure all the kids were feeling grounded and secure and loved and involved.

The insider claimed: "Now, as the 40 days are nearly up, Kourtney says she's going to take her time getting back to it."
