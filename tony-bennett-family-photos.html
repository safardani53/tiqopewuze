Tony Bennetts Family: Photos Hollywood Life

Tony Bennett and his wife, actress Sandra Grant with their two children, Antonia (3) And Joanna (7) at the Art Exhibition At The Milne-Henderson Gallery. They were dressed up as they sat together and posed for a camera.

Tony was a legendary jazz singer who stepped onto the music scene in the 1930s and stayed there until around 2021. He made quite a lasting impression in the industry and won many awards throughout the years, including 20 Grammy Awards, a Lifetime Achievement Award, and two Primetime Emmy Awards. He died in Jully 2023 and left behind an incredible legacy.

In addition to his music career, Tony was known for having a loving family and was married multiple times in his life. His former spouses include Patricia Beech from 1952 until 1971, Sandra Grant from 1971 until 1983, and Susan Crow, whom he married in 2007. In addition to Susan, he left behind four children. They include Danny and Dae Bennett, who he shared with Patricia, and Joanna and Antonia, who he shared with Sandra.
