EastEnders' Natalie Cassidy shares rare picture of her daughters and husband during night out in Lon

NATALIE Cassidy has given her followers a rare glimpse into her family life.

The 40-year-old actress, who is best known for starring in EastEnders, enjoyed a night-out in London with her fiancé Marc and her two daughters, Eliza and Joanie.

In the adorable family photo, the four could be seen smiling and chatting as the snap was taken.

The whole family appeared to be dressed up as they gathered around a table and enjoyed hot chocolate.

Natalie, who has played Sonia Fowler since 1993, recently posed with Marc on a festive date night.

She also shared an insight into her jaw-dropping Christmas decorations at her Hertfordshire home.

The front of the house was adorned with a giant Christmas wreath, accompanied by two smaller frosted wreathes.

Natalie captioned the post: "Just wanted to say a big thank you to Hayley @thedoordisplaycompany for another beautiful door.

"Every season she and her sister Cassie come over and create their magic displays.

"We have a laugh and a good catch up and they leave happy with their work and I get left with joy."
