Wilfredo Gómez Rivera, sometimes known as Bazooka Gómez, is a former Puerto Rican professional boxer and three-time world champion. He is regularly mentioned among the best Puerto Rican boxers of all time, along with Félix Trinidad, Miguel Cotto, Wilfred Benítez, Esteban De Jesús, Edwin Rosario and Carlos Ortíz. His seventeen consecutive knockouts in championship defenders is a record for any boxing division. He is ranked 13th on The Ring magazine's "100 Greatest Fists of All Time" list. In 1995, Gómez was placed in the International Boxing Hall of Fame.
