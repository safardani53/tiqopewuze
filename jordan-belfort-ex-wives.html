Who are Jordan Belfort's ex wives?

STOCKBROKER Jordan Belfort is notoriously known for his extravagant lifestyle and crimes in connection with the stock market. 

It’s fair to say his life has been anything but boring, especially when it comes to women. So let's take a look at who his ex wives are… 

Who are Jordan Belfort's ex wives? 

Denise Lombardo

Denise Lombardo is famous as Belfort’s first wife, she was born on November 11, 1962, in Ohio.

Denise was born into an Italian family to her father Anthony Florito and mother, Ann Lombardo.

After high school she graduated and went to Adelphi University where she obtained a bachelor’s degree in English literature.

Denise and Belfort met while they were both attending Bayside High School together.

They began dating and eventually went from childhood sweethearts to a married couple.

They decided to have an intimate wedding ceremony in 1985, this was during the time Belfort was struggling financially as he had just announced bankruptcy.

Jordan began his company Stratton Oakmont, which reached an unexpected height of success.

Belfort chose a lifestyle of drugs, affairs and extravagant parties. 

Denise was still supportive of Belfort and their marriage till his affair with Nadine.

Denise was left heartbroken and the pair separated in 1991.

Nadine Caridi

Nadine, currently known as Nadine Macaluso, is Jordan Belfort’s second wife.

She met Belfort at the end of the 80s, while he was still married to his first wife.

Caridi was introduced to Belfort by her ex-boyfriend, Alan Wilzig.

Wilzig wrote on his Facebook during the time The Wolf of Wall Street movie came out, saying: "Twenty-odd years ago while ‘The Wolf’ was still growing his fangs — I had my first post-college relationship,” 

"At the end of year 2, as friendly-exes I took Nadine to Jordan Belfort’s July 4th Westhampton Beach House party where the two of them met… He left his high school sweetheart wife to marry her; and the rest you can see in the movie."

Caridi married Belfort in 1991 and separated in 1998. They had two children, Chandler and Carter.

Margot Robbie played the role of Belfort's wife in the movie - The Wolf Of Wall Street.

Margot explained how it was not intended to be a true-to-life portrayal of Caridi.

However, Caridi shared some similarities with her on-screen persona.

Margot got the chance to speak with Caridi before filming to understand her marriage with Belfort.

She told IndieWire: ‘When I asked what they would fight about, [Caridi] was like, ‘The drugs. The fact that he was a drug addict… I didn’t care about the hookers or coming home late.’ … She said he would be doing crack in front of their newborn baby. Any mother would divorce their husband for that."

There's little information about what divorce settlement Caridi received.

Although Belfort gave a clue when he spoke to Notable Life, saying: "Sign a prenup, Don’t get yourself in a situation where if you get divorced you will screw yourself."

Caridi moved to California with the children and had no financial support from Belfort.

When she was 39 she earned herself two degrees in Counseling and a PhD. in Somatic and Depth psychology.

She is now married to John Macaluso with whom she shares a blended family.

The couple have been together for 22 years.

Anne Koppe

Anne Koppe reportedly married Belfort in 2008, but the marriage didn't last.

Most outlets report that the couple were not married but in his podcast Belfort refers to her as his ex wife.

In his podcast he states they were together for 12 years before they amicably split - and he's still a huge fan of his ex wife.

Belfort and Koppe are business partners.

Is Jordan Belfort currently married? 

Jordan is not currently married. 

As of September 2021 Belfort is dating model Cristina Invernizzi.

The pair met for the first time in a pub in Mexico and have been officially dating since 2019.

Does Jordan Belfort have kids?

Belfort has two children from his marriage with Nadine.
