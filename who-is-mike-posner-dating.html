Mike Posner Biography

Mike Posner was born on a Friday, February 12, 1988 in Detroit, MI. His birth name is Michael Robert Posner and he is currently 35 years old. People born on February 12 fall under the zodiac sign of Aquarius. His zodiac animal is Dragon.

Michael Robert Henrion “Mike” Posner is an American singer, songwriter, and record producer. Posner released his debut album, 31 Minutes to Takeoff, on August 10, 2010. The album includes the US Billboard Hot 100 top 10 single “Cooler than Me” as well as the top 20 single “Please Don’t Go”. In 2016, a remix of his 2015 single “I Took a Pill in Ibiza” peaked at number 10 on the Billboard Hot 100. He is also known for writing “Sugar” by Maroon 5, “Boyfriend” by Justin Bieber, “Beneath Your Beautiful” by Labrinth, as well as songs for Talib Kweli, Pharrell Williams, Big Sean, 2 Chainz, Wiz Khalifa, Nelly, TI, Snoop Dogg, Nick Jonas, Avicii, Austin Mahone, and more.

He attended Duke University and earned a degree in sociology.
