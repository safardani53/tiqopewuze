How much does Swoon's delivery cost?

Swoon's delivery prices vary depending on your shopping basket's total.

There's a £4 charge for orders of up to £49.99, and for orders that are priced between £50 and £149.99 you'd have to pay £8 delivery charge.

For larger orders between £150 and £299.99 there's a £15 delivery cost.

You can find out more information about Swoon's delivery here.

Swoon also states that with medium-to-large items its delivery partners will take the item to the chosen room and, in some cases, help to assemble for you.
