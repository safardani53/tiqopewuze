Araksya Karapetyan Career & Net Worth

Born on August 27, 1982, in Gyumri, Armenia, Araksya Karapetyan has studied at S.I. Newhouse School Of Public Communications in New York. She received a degree in international relations and broadcast journalism from there.

She started her television career as an intern at KABC-TV in Los Angeles and then worked at Burbank-based KFI 640 AM radio.

She has since worked as a reporter and host for KOIN-TV in Portland, Oregon, and as a reporter, anchor, and producer for KIDK-TV in Idaho Falls, Idaho.

She also served as a reporter at CitiCABLE in Torrance, California, which is a government access cable television channel.

She joined the KTTV Channel in 2012 after which she rose to fame. As of 2019, Araksya is currently hosting the Good Day L.A. in Los Angeles which airs every morning.

Araksya, who earns approx. $36 per hour and approx. $50,000 per year, has an estimated net worth of $5 million.
